# Install & Activate FluentBooking Pro

This guide walks through installing **FluentBooking Pro** on your WordPress site—download the plugin, upload it from the WordPress admin, and activate your license key.

> Looking for the free version? See [Install & Activate FluentBooking](/install-activate-and-update-fluentbooking) instead. You can install Pro on top of an existing free install without losing any data.

::: tip Important
You need an active FluentBooking Pro license. Purchase one from [fluentbooking.com](https://fluentbooking.com/) and have your **WP Manage Ninja** account credentials ready.
:::

## Download the Zip File

Log in to your [**WP Manage Ninja Dashboard**](https://wpmanageninja.com/account/dashboard/) with the credentials you used at purchase.

Open the **Downloads** section from the left sidebar. Find **FluentBooking Pro** in the list and click the **Download** button. A `fluent-booking-pro.zip` file will save to your device.

Next, open the **Licenses** section and copy your **License Key**. You will need it in the final step.

![License Key](/images/getting-started/how-to-install-activate-fluent-booking/booking-licence-1-scaled.webp)

## Install the Plugin

In your WordPress admin, go to **Plugins → Add New Plugin** and click **Upload Plugin** at the top of the page.

Click **Choose File**, select the `fluent-booking-pro.zip` you just downloaded, and click **Install Now**. When the upload finishes, click **Activate Plugin**.

![Installing FluentBooking Pro](/images/getting-started/how-to-install-activate-fluent-booking/install-fb.webp)

## Activate the License Key

From the FluentBooking admin, go to **Settings** and open the **License** section in the left sidebar. Paste the license key you copied from your WP Manage Ninja account.

Click **Verify License**. FluentBooking Pro is now activated and all Pro features are unlocked.

![Activating the License Key](/images/getting-started/how-to-install-activate-fluent-booking/booking-licence-2-scaled.webp)
