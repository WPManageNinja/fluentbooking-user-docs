# Introduction to FluentBooking

**FluentBooking** is a powerful, native WordPress scheduling plugin built to replace bloated SaaS booking tools. Run appointments, sales calls, consultations, webinars, and team events directly from your own site—no monthly per-seat fees, no data leaving your server.

This guide gives you a quick tour of what FluentBooking can do and where to go next.

## Why FluentBooking?

- **100% WordPress-native.** Your data, your domain, your branding.
- **Lightning fast.** Built on a modern Vue.js dashboard with a lightweight footprint.
- **Unlimited everything.** Create unlimited hosts, events, and bookings on every plan.
- **Made by the WPManageNinja team.** From the makers of Fluent Forms, FluentCRM, and Fluent Support.

## Schedule Anything, for Anyone

Whether you are a solo consultant or a multi-host team, FluentBooking adapts to how you work. Each host gets their own public slug and reusable weekly schedule.

Supported event types:

- **One-to-One** — classic 1:1 appointments and consultations.
- **Group Events** — webinars, classes, and workshops with capacity limits.
- **[Round Robin](/host-events/how-to-set-round-robin-bookings#set-round-robin-bookings)** — distribute bookings evenly across a team.
- **[Collective](/host-events/collective-booking-event#create-collective-booking)** — book multiple hosts together for a single meeting.
- **Recurring & One-off Events** — fixed-date sessions or repeating series.

## Total Control Over Availability

Stay in control of your calendar with granular scheduling rules:

- **Buffer times** before and after meetings.
- **Minimum notice** and **daily cut-offs** to block last-minute bookings.
- **Booking limits** per day, week, or month.
- **Future range** to cap how far ahead invitees can book.
- **Date overrides** for holidays and one-off availability changes.

## A Seamless Booking Experience

Invitees pick a slot, fill in your form, and get instant confirmation—all without leaving your site. Embed the booking widget anywhere with:

- **Shortcodes** — drop a calendar, team page, or single event into any page or post.
- **Gutenberg Blocks** — native blocks inside the WordPress editor.
- **Elementor Widgets** — first-class support for Elementor layouts.
- **HTML Embed** — paste into any external site.

## Pro Power-Ups

Upgrade to **FluentBooking Pro** to unlock:

- **Two-way calendar sync** with Google, Outlook, Apple, and Nextcloud—no double bookings.
- **Payments & deposits** via Stripe, PayPal, WooCommerce, or offline methods.
- **Coupons & discounts** with single-use links and per-event rules.
- **Automated meeting links** for Zoom and Google Meet on every booking.
- **SMS & WhatsApp reminders** through Twilio.
- **Webhooks** to push booking events anywhere.

## Automate Your Growth

Connect FluentBooking to the rest of your stack and let it run on autopilot:

- **FluentCRM** — auto-tag contacts, trigger sequences, and segment by event.
- **Fluent Forms** — capture richer booking data with conditional fields.
- **FluentBoards** — turn bookings into project tasks.
- **FluentCart & WooCommerce** — sell paid sessions like products.
- **Reports Dashboard** — track bookings, completion rates, revenue, and guests.
