# Nextcloud Calendar Integration With FluentBooking

FluentBooking easily connects with Nextcloud Calendar, so you can sync your booking events hassle-free. Just follow a few simple steps to integrate FluentBooking with Nextcloud Calendar.


## Enabling Nextcloud Calendar


To enable Nextcloud Calendar go to your FluentBooking**Settings**and here you will find your**Nextcloud Calendar**settings in the left sidebar. Now click on the Checkbox to enable the**Nextcloud Calendar.**Also, you have to give here the**Nextcloud CalDav Primary URL**.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/0.webp)


### **Nextcloud CalDav Primary URL**


Login to your**Nextcloud Developer [Account](https://nextcloud.com/sign-up/)** to get the**Primary CalDav URL**. Now select**Calendar**from your Nextcloud account and click on the**Calendar Settings**.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/1-1.webp)


Now Scroll down to get the**CalDav Primary URL**and copy the**URL**from here.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/2-1.webp)


Paste the**URL**on your FluentBooking**Nextcloud Calendar Settings**then click on the**Save Settings**button like the below screenshot.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/3-1.webp)


## **Nextcloud Calendar for Host**


After enabling your Nextcloud Calendar, you need to add your Nextcloud Calendar to your**Host**profile.


Go to your**Host settings**of the host for whom you want to add the Nextcloud Calendar.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-1.webp)


Now select the**Remote Calendar**from the left sidebar and select the**Connect with Nextcloud Calendar**button.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-2.webp)


You will need the**Nextcloud ID (Username/Email)**and an**App Specific Password**here. To get these credentials you need to go to your Nextcloud Account again.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-3.webp)


### **Nextcloud Calendar App Credentials**


Login to your [Nextcloud Account](https://nextcloud.com/sign-up/) and go to your**Nextcloud Profile Settings.**


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-4.webp)


Select the**Security**from the left sidebar.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-5.webp)


Scroll down and go to the**Devices & Sessions**section, you have to create an**App**here. Give the**App**name and click on the**Create new app password**button.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-6.webp)


You will get your**Nextcloud ID (Username/Email)**and**App Specific Password**here. Copy these credentials and click on the**Done**button.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-7.webp)


Paste the credentials to your FluentBooking host’s Remote Calendar settings and click on the**Connect with Nextcloud Calendar**button.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-8.webp)


Now select the Nextcloud calendar to add the booking events on the selected calendar.


![](/images/integrations/next-cloud-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-9.webp)


And that’s it. Your FluentBooking integration with Nextcloud Calendar is done.
