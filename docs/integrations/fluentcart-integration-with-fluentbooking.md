# FluentCart Integration With FluentBooking

**[FluentCart](https://fluentcart.com/)** is built for speed, scalability, and flexibility, helping you run your eCommerce business smoothly and efficiently. With this integration, you can sell consultations, classes, services, or products directly through your booking forms without requiring third-party plugins.

This keeps your sales and appointments within a single, seamless ecosystem without needing extra plugins.Follow these steps to connect FluentCart with your booking events:

## Activate the FluentCart Module

First, ensure FluentCart is installed and active on your WordPress site.

Log in to your WordPress dashboard. Navigate to **FluentBooking** and select **Settings** from the top menu bar.

Next, click on **"Advanced Features & Addons."** Here, you will see the system automatically enabled once you install and activate the FluentCart plugin.

![Activate the FluentCart Module](/images/integrations/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/activate-the-fluentcart-module-scaled.webp)

## Configure FluentCart Inside Your Booking Event

Once the module is active, you need to connect it to a specific calendar event.

Go to **Booking Calendars** from your FluentBooking dashboard. Click the **Edit** button on the specific event you want to monetize. If you haven’t created a booking event yet, click on the **+New** button.

![Configure FluentCart Inside Your Booking Event](/images/integrations/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/edit-calendar-02-scaled.webp)

### Set Up the Product and Checkout

From the left sidebar menu, select **Payment Settings**. Then, check the box to enable **"Enable this payment as Paid and collect payment on booking**''.

Under **Payment Method**, select **FluentCart Checkout** from the dropdown menu.

**Select a Product:** You will see a dropdown list of your existing FluentCart products. Select the appropriate product for this service.

>[!Note]
> If you don't have a product yet, click **+ New** to create one directly from this screen.
>
>
> **Free Version Note:** Users on the free version can connect to only **one product**.

**Multiple Durations (Optional):** If you have set up multiple meeting durations for your event, you can assign different products and prices to specific durations. Click the **Save Changes** button to finalize the setup.

![Multiple Durations](/images/integrations/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/payment-settings-03-scaled.webp)

### Booking Order and Payment Confirmation

Once configured, your invitees will see a price displayed on your booking form that matches the connected FluentCart product.

![Booking Order and Payment Confirmation](/images/integrations/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/event-4.webp)

**The User Experience:** When an invitee selects a time and proceeds, the entire payment flow is handled via the **FluentCart checkout page**. This ensures a secure and familiar payment experience for your clients.

![FluentCart checkout page](/images/integrations/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/checkout-5.webp)

**Order Completion:** After the order is completed, the booking is fully processed. The payment is recorded via the FluentCart method you configured.

![Order Completion](/images/integrations/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/receipt-6.webp)

### Managing Bookings and Orders

All bookings made through this integration are automatically synced between the two plugins.

**In FluentBooking,** you can view the appointment details in the **Bookings** section.

![Managing Bookings](/images/integrations/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/booking-details-6-7-scaled.webp)

**In FluentCart:** Each booking is recorded as an official **Order**, ensuring your sales data and customer records remain centralized in one ecosystem.

![Order details](/images/integrations/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking/order-details-7-scaled.webp)


**Congratulations!** You have successfully integrated FluentCart with FluentBooking, turning your appointments into a fully connected WooCommerce-powered sales channel.
