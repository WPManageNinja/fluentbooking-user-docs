# Outlook Calendar Integration with FluentBooking

Integrating your **Outlook Calendar** with FluentBooking allows you to automatically sync your booking events with your Outlook schedule. This helps you manage appointments more efficiently and keeps your availability up to date.

Follow the step-by-step guide below to connect your Outlook Calendar and start syncing your booking events seamlessly.

## Select Your Calendar

Start by selecting the **Calendar** you want to integrate and access its **Host settings**.

![Select Your Calendar](/images/integrations/outlook-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/1-2.webp)

## Connect with Outlook Calendar

Choose the **Remote Calendar** now, where you'll find the **Outlook Calendar** option. Click on the **Connect with Outlook Calendar** button.

![Connect with Outlook Calendar](/images/integrations/outlook-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/2-2.webp)

## Log in to Your Outlook Account

You will be prompted to log in to your **Microsoft Outlook** account. **Enter** your login credentials to continue.

![Log in to Your Outlook Account](/images/integrations/outlook-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/3-2.webp)

After logging in, grant access permission by clicking on the **Accept** button.

![Grant Permission](/images/integrations/outlook-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/4-2.webp)

Once approved, your Outlook account will be successfully connected.

## Outlook Calendar Account Selection

You can add multiple Outlook accounts to sync your booking events on your Outlook Calendar. Just click on the **Add** button to add another new account and follow the above steps.

![ Outlook Calendar Account Selection](/images/integrations/outlook-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/10.webp)

Choose the **Outlook Calendar** account from the **Create Events On** section in which you intend to schedule an event when you are booked.

Enable the calendar checkbox you wish to monitor for conflicts to avoid double bookings.

![Create Events On](/images/integrations/outlook-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/11.webp)

## Customize Caching Time

To customize the caching time for Outlook Calendar events, navigate to **Settings > Outlook Calendar**. From the drop-down menu, select your preferred caching time. We recommend a caching time of 5 to 10 minutes. Adjust this value if you frequently add manual events to your Outlook Calendar.

![Customize Caching Time](/images/integrations/outlook-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/6.webp)

Congratulations, your **Outlook Calendar** Integration is finished!
