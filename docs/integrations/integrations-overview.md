# Integrations Overview

FluentBooking integrations help you connect your booking workflow with calendars, video meetings, CRM, automation tools, and payment platforms. Use this page to quickly understand what each integration does and open the right setup guide.

> The integrations listed on this page are available in FluentBooking Pro. You will need the [FluentBooking Pro](https://fluentbooking.com/pricing/) plugin to use them.

## Calendar integrations

Use calendar integrations to sync events, avoid conflicts, and keep your schedule updated across platforms.

- **[Google Calendar & Meet](/integrations/google-calendar-meet-integration-with-fluent-booking)**: Sync bookings and automatically create Google Meet links.
- **[Outlook Calendar](/integrations/outlook-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking)**: Connect Microsoft calendars for event sync and availability checks.
- **[Apple Calendar](/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking)**: Sync bookings with Apple Calendar using app-specific credentials.
- **[Nextcloud Calendar](/integrations/nextcloud-calendar-integration-with-fluentbooking)**: Connect CalDAV-based Nextcloud calendars for host-level scheduling.

## Video meeting integrations

Use meeting integrations to generate conferencing links automatically when someone books an event.

- **[Zoom](/integrations/zoom-integration-with-fluentbooking)**: Create and manage Zoom meeting links from FluentBooking.
- **[Google Calendar & Meet](/integrations/google-calendar-meet-integration-with-fluent-booking)**: Add Google Meet links directly to booking events.

## CRM and workflow integrations

Use these integrations to send booking data to your marketing and operations stack.

- **[FluentCRM](/integrations/fluentcrm-integration-with-fluentbooking)**: Add attendees as contacts, apply tags, and trigger automations.
- **[Fluent Forms](/integrations/fluent-form-integration-with-fluentbooking)**: Collect enriched booking details with form fields and dynamic data.
- **[FluentBoards](/integrations/fluentboards-integration-with-fluentbooking)**: Create and manage board tasks from booking activity.
- **[Webhooks](/integrations/webhook-integration-with-fluentbooking)**: Send booking events to any external API endpoint.

## Commerce and notifications integrations

Use commerce and communication integrations to manage paid bookings and booking alerts.

- **[WooCommerce](/integrations/woocommerce-integration-with-fluentbooking)**: Sell booking events as products with WooCommerce checkout.
- **[FluentCart](/integrations/fluentcart-integration-with-fluentbooking)**: Handle booking payments using FluentCart workflows.
- **[Twilio SMS & WhatsApp](/integrations/twilio-integration-with-fluentbooking)**: Send booking notifications through SMS and WhatsApp.

## Recommended setup order

If you are setting up integrations for the first time, follow this order:

1. Configure one **calendar integration** for availability sync.
2. Set your **video meeting integration** for online events.
3. Connect your **CRM or automation integration** for follow-up workflows.
4. Enable **commerce and notifications** for payments and reminders.
