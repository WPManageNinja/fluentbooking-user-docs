# Apple Calendar Integration with FluentBooking

You can easily integrate your Apple Calendar with FluentBooking without any hassle. After integrating your Apple Calendar with FluentBooking you can monitor and keep track of your events from your Apple devices. Please follow the following steps to configure Apple Calendar with FluentBooking.

## Enable Apple Calendar Integration

Go to your **Settings** first then select the **Apple Calendar** from the left sidebar. Enable the **Apple Calendar**, and set the caching time to 5-10 minutes for optimal performance. Click the **Save Settings** button.

![Enable Apple Calendar](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/enable-apple-calendar-1.webp)

Now, go to the **Calendar** and the **Host Settings** of the host for whom you want to add Apple Calendar.

![Host Settings](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/host-settings-2.webp)

Select the **Remote Settings** then click the **Connect with Apple Calendar** button.

![Connect with Apple Calendar](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/remote-calendar-3.webp)

Now a pop-up will come where you must give the host's **Apple ID (Email)** and the **App Specific Password.**

![App Specific Password](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/add-specific-password-4.webp)

## Host's Apple ID and App-Specific Password

To get the App-Specific Password login to [**Apple Account**](https://appleid.apple.com/account/manage) and you will see the **App-Specific Password** there click on it.

![App-Specific Password login ](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/login-5.webp)

A pop-up will come to ask for confirmation of your App creation.

![confirmation of your App creation](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/app-creation-6.webp)

Give your App a name and click on the **Create** button. Now you will be asked for your **Apple Account Password**.

![Create Apple Account](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/create-apple-7.webp)

Then you will get your **App-specific Password**. **Copy** the password from here.

![Copy Password](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/copy-8.webp)

## Connecting to the Apple Calendar

Now paste the App-Specific Password here and click the **Connect with Apple Calendar** button.

![Connecting the Apple Calendar](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/connect-9.webp)

Select the **Calendar** here and you are done.

![Select the Calendar](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/calendar-10.webp)

Now you will see your bookings on Apple Calendar like this.

![Apple Calendar](/images/integrations/apple-calendar-integration-with-fluent-booking/apple-calendar-11.webp)
